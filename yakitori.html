Yakitori

Price: R 55,00

Serving: 4

Yakitori is a Japanese type of skewered pork or chicken. Its preparation involves skewering the meat with kushi, a type of skewer typically made of steel, bamboo, or similar materials. Afterwards, they are grilled over a charcoal fire. During or after cooking, the meat is typically seasoned with tare sauce or salt.
