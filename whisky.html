Whisky No Kaze

Price: R 30,00

Serving: 1

If you enjoy flavours as complex as the oldest of whiskies, the Whisky No Kaze (meaning 'The Wind of Whisky') is the cocktail for you. The alcohol base of this cocktail blends the herby, dynamic flavours of namazake, green chartreuse, and hakushu whisky. This is then complimented with the citrus flavours of orange and lime, the freshness of mint leaves, and the sweetness of vanilla syrup. Try it and see if you can taste all the flavours within.
