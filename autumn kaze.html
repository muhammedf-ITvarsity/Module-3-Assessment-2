Experience how the unique flavours of Japanese alcohols lend themselves well to mixed drinks with this recipe for an 'autumn kaze' cocktail. This shochu-based drink sings of autumn with its warming coffee, vanilla and honey flavours that are then subtly highlighted with refreshing orange and yuzu citrus. Ideal for serving at cocktail parties.
